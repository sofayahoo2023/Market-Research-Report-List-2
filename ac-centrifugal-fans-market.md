<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The AC centrifugal fans market can be classified into three types: forward-curved, backward-curved, and straight radial fans. </p><p>Forward-curved fans are designed with blades that curve away from the direction of rotation. These fans are efficient and commonly used in applications where high airflow is required, such as cooling systems.</p><p>Backward-curved fans have blades that curve towards the direction of rotation. They are known for their high efficiency and are commonly used in applications that require high pressure and low airflow, such as HVAC systems.</p><p>Straight radial fans have blades that are straight and parallel to the axis of rotation. They are commonly used in applications that require a combination of high pressure and high airflow, such as air filtration systems.</p><p>Overall, these different types of AC centrifugal fans cater to various needs in terms of airflow, pressure, and efficiency.</p></p>
