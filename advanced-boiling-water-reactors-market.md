They can be categorized into two market types: Single Cycle Steam Generation and Dual Cycle Steam Generation. In Single Cycle Steam Generation, the reactor produces high-pressure steam, which is used directly to drive the turbine for electricity generation. In Dual Cycle Steam Generation, the reactor produces both high-pressure steam for electricity generation and low-pressure steam for other industrial processes, thus increasing the overall efficiency and utilization of the reactor's thermal output.</p></p>
