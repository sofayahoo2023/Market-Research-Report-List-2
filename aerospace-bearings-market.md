<p><strong>The Aerospace Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller</li><li>Ball</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace bearings market is categorized into three types: roller bearings, ball bearings, and others. Roller bearings consist of cylindrical, tapered, and needle roller bearings that can withstand high radial and axial loads. Ball bearings involve the use of small metal balls between the inner and outer races to reduce friction and support both radial and axial loads. The "others" category includes specialty bearings like spherical bearings and self-lubricating bearings, which offer unique features suitable for specific aerospace applications. These different types of bearings cater to diverse aerospace industry requirements.</p></p>
