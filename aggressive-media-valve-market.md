<p><p>The aggressive media valve market comprises 2-way and 3-way valves. A 2-way valve controls media flow in two directions, allowing or blocking the passage of liquids or gases. This type is commonly used in aggressive environments where the media is corrosive or hazardous. Similarly, a 3-way valve also controls media flow but has an additional port, enabling diversion or mixing of media in various directions. These valves find applications in industries dealing with aggressive fluids or gases, offering reliable and safe operation in challenging conditions.</p></p>
