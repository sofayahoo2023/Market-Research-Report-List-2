<p><p>The air heaters market comprises various types of heaters used for different applications. Indirect fired air heaters are designed to prevent direct contact between the combustion gases and the air, making them suitable for heating sensitive materials. Direct fired air heaters directly expose the air to combustion gases, making them ideal for industrial heating processes. Duct heaters are used to heat airflow in duct systems for residential and commercial purposes. Portable air heaters provide temporary heating solutions for small spaces or outdoor activities. Lastly, "others" include specialized air heaters for specific industries or unique applications.</p></p>
