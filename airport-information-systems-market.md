<p><p>Airport Information Systems (AIS) encompass various types of technology solutions that aid in the efficient operations and management of airports. Resource Management Systems allocate and monitor airport assets like gates and parking spaces. Flight Information Display Systems (FIDS) provide real-time flight information to passengers. Passenger Processing Systems streamline check-in, security, and boarding procedures. Airport Operations Systems handle functions such as security, staffing, and maintenance. Public Address Systems broadcast important announcements throughout the airport. Baggage Processing Systems automate the handling of luggage. Other AIS solutions include revenue management systems, self-service kiosks, and airport management software.</p></p>
