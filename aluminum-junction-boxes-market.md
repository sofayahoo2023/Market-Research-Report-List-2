<p><strong>The Aluminum Junction Boxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IP66</li><li>IP54</li><li>IP67</li><li>IP55</li><li>IP65</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum junction boxes market includes various types distinguished by their IP (Ingress Protection) ratings. IP66 boxes are highly dust tight and protected against powerful water jets. IP54 boxes offer limited dust ingress protection and protection against water splashes. IP67 boxes are fully dust tight and can be immersed in water up to 1 meter. IP55 boxes provide limited dust and water ingress protection. IP65 boxes are dust proof and protected against low-pressure water jets. These ratings indicate the level of protection offered by the junction boxes against solids and liquids.</p></p>
