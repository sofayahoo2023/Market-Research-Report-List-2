<p><p>The Anaesthesia Information Management System (AIMS) market can be divided into two main types: B/S framework and C/S framework. B/S framework refers to systems that are browser-based, meaning they can be accessed through a web browser. C/S framework, on the other hand, stands for client/server framework, where the system is accessed through a dedicated client application installed on a device. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages, but ultimately serve the purpose of managing and monitoring anaesthesia-related information efficiently and securely.</p></p>
