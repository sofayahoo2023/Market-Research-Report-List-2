<p><p>Anti-viral masks can be categorized into two types: flat-fold and cup style. Flat-fold masks are designed to lay flat on the face, covering the nose, mouth, and chin. They are easier to store, transport, and dispose of. On the other hand, cup style masks have a three-dimensional shape, providing a larger breathing space. They offer a more secure fit and are often preferred by healthcare professionals. Both types play a crucial role in protecting users from viral particles and are essential in preventing the spread of infectious diseases.</p></p>
