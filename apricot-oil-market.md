<p><strong>The Apricot Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Extraction Method</li><li>Cold Pressed Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Apricot oil is commonly extracted using two methods: solvent extraction and cold pressing. Solvent extraction involves using chemical solvents to extract the oil from the apricot kernels, resulting in a higher yield but with the possibility of chemical residue. On the other hand, the cold pressing method uses a mechanical process to crush the kernels and extract the oil without using heat or chemicals, preserving its natural qualities. Both types have their own advantages and market demand depends on consumer preferences for either higher yield or a more natural product.</p></p>
