<p><p>Aromatherapy diffusers are devices that disperse essential oils into the air for therapeutic purposes. There are three main types in the market. Ultrasonic diffusers use water to create a fine mist of essential oils through high-frequency vibrations. Nebulizer diffusers pressurize and release essential oils directly into the air without the use of water or heat. Evaporative diffusers utilize a fan or heat source to evaporate the oils and disperse them into the surrounding space. Each type of diffuser offers a different method of diffusing essential oils, catering to varying preferences and needs.</p></p>
