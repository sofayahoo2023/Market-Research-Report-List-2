<p><p>The market for ASD occluder devices can be classified into two types - Single-Fenestrated ASD Occluders and Multi-Fenestrated ASD Occluders. Both types of devices are commonly employed in the treatment of atrial septal defects (ASDs), a condition characterized by abnormal openings in the heart's septum.</p></p>
