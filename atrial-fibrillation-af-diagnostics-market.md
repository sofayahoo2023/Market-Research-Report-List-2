<p><p>The Atrial Fibrillation (AF) Diagnostics Market includes several types of diagnostic tests. Electrocardiogram (ECG) measures the electrical activity of the heart. Echocardiogram (echo) uses sound waves to create images of the heart. Transesophageal Echocardiography (TEE) provides more detailed images of the heart. Chest X-ray gives a clear view of the heart and lungs. Blood tests analyze certain biomarkers to identify AF. Stress testing measures heart activity during physical exertion. Electrical activity testing assesses the heart's electrical signals. These tests are crucial in diagnosing and monitoring AF patients.</p></p>
