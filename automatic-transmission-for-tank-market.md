<p><p>In the tank market, there are two types of automatic transmission systems: Single Stage Type and Multi Stage Type. The Single Stage Type transmission refers to a system that uses only one set of gears to transmit power from the engine to the tracks. On the other hand, the Multi Stage Type transmission utilizes multiple sets of gears, allowing for a wider range of gear ratios and improved efficiency. These different types of transmission systems play a crucial role in determining the performance and capabilities of tanks in various operational conditions.</p></p>
