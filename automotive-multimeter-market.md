<p><p>The automotive multimeter market is mainly comprised of two types: digital and analog multimeters. Digital multimeters utilize a digital display to provide accurate measurements and are equipped with advanced features like auto-ranging and data logging. They are user-friendly and preferred for their precision and versatility. On the other hand, analog multimeters have a needle-like pointer on a scale to indicate readings and are generally considered as traditional devices. While less common nowadays, they are still used by some technicians who prefer their simplicity and quick response time.</p></p>
