<p><p>Automotive pressure transducers are devices used to measure and monitor pressure in automotive systems. There are four common types of pressure in the automotive pressure transducer market: gauge pressure, absolute pressure, sealed pressure, and vacuum pressure. </p><p>Gauge pressure transducers measure the pressure relative to atmospheric pressure. Absolute pressure transducers measure the pressure relative to a perfect vacuum. Sealed pressure transducers are designed to measure pressures within a sealed system, while vacuum pressure transducers measure pressures below atmospheric pressure. Each type of transducer serves a specific purpose in automotive applications, allowing for accurate pressure monitoring and control.</p></p>
