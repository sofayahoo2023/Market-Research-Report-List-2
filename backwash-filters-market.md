<p><p>Backwash filters are used to remove impurities from liquids and gases. There are two main types: manual backwash filters and automatic backwash filters. </p><p>Manual backwash filters require human intervention to initiate the cleaning process. This could involve turning a handle or a valve to reverse the flow and flush out the accumulated contaminants. </p><p>On the other hand, automatic backwash filters are designed to self-clean without any manual intervention. They are equipped with automated mechanisms that periodically reverse the flow or activate internal cleaning mechanisms to remove the impurities.</p></p>
