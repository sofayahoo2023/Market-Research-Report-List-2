<p><ul><li>Direct Feed</li><li>Indirect Feed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bank feed market types are categorized into direct feed and indirect feed. In a direct feed market, banks directly provide financial data to third-party applications, ensuring real-time, accurate information for users. Contrarily, an indirect feed market relies on data aggregators that gather information from various sources, including banks. While indirect feeds offer broader compatibility, they may be subject to delays or inaccuracies due to data sourcing. Both markets aim to enhance financial management by automating transactions and providing relevant insights, but direct feeds prioritize reliability while indirect feeds prioritize breadth of access.</p></p>
