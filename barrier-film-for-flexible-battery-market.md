<p><p>Barrier film is a protective layer used in flexible batteries to prevent moisture and oxygen from reaching the battery components, ensuring their longevity and performance. Multilayer Barrier (MLB) film technology involves the use of multiple layers of different materials, each with specific barrier properties, to create an effective barrier against moisture and oxygen. Thin Film Encapsulation (TFE) technology, on the other hand, uses a single layer of advanced materials, such as inorganic compounds, to form a highly efficient barrier. Both technologies offer effective solutions for protecting flexible batteries and enhancing their durability.</p></p>
