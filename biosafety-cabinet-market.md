<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Biosafety Cabinet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class II A Type</li><li>Class II B Type</li><li>Class III Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biosafety cabinet market comprises different types such as Class II A Type, Class II B Type, and Class III Type cabinets. Class II A Type cabinets are partially recirculating cabinets that provide personnel, product, and environmental protection. Class II B Type cabinets are total exhaust cabinets providing maximum protection to personnel, products, and the environment. Class III Type cabinets are totally enclosed cabinets under negative pressure, designed for working with high-risk infectious materials. These different types cater to the varying levels of safety requirements and are utilized in different research and diagnostic settings.</p></p>
