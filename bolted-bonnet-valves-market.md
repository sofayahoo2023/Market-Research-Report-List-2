<p><p>Bolted bonnet valves are a type of industrial valves used to control the flow of fluids in various applications. The market for bolted bonnet valves can be categorized into three types: bolted bonnet check valves, bolted bonnet globe valves, and bolted bonnet gate valves. Bolted bonnet check valves are designed to allow fluid flow in one direction while preventing backflow. Bolted bonnet globe valves have a spherical body shape and are used for regulating flow. Bolted bonnet gate valves have a sliding gate that controls fluid flow, making them ideal for on/off applications.</p></p>
