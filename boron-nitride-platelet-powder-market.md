<p><p>The Boron Nitride Platelet Powder market consists of two main types: Spherical Agglomerates (SA) and High Density Agglomerates (HD). On the other hand, HD refers to Boron Nitride powder that is compressed under high pressure to form dense agglomerates, resulting in improved mechanical properties and thermal conductivity.
