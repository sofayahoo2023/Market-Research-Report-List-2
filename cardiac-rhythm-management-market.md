<p><p>The Cardiac Rhythm Management market includes various types of devices used for managing heart rhythm disorders. Implantable Cardiac Rhythm Devices are small devices that are surgically implanted in the body and continuously monitor and regulate the heart's rhythm. These devices include implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs) and cardiac resynchronization therapy devices (CRTs). Pacemakers, a subset of implantable cardiac rhythm devices, help regulate the heartbeat by generating electrical impulses. They are commonly used for patients with slow or irregular heart rhythms. These devices play a crucial role in managing cardiac rhythm disorders and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
