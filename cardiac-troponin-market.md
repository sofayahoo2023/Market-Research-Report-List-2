<p><p>Cardiac Troponin is a protein found in the heart muscles that plays a crucial role in regulating cardiac muscle contraction. It consists of three subunits, namely Troponin C, Troponin T, and Troponin I. When the heart muscle is damaged, such as during a heart attack, cardiac troponin is released into the bloodstream. Therefore, measuring the levels of cardiac troponin in the blood is a reliable and sensitive method for diagnosing and assessing the severity of heart-related conditions.</p><p>The Cardiac Troponin Market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period.
