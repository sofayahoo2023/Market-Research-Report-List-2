<p><p>A cavity coupler, also known as a waveguide coupler or power coupler, is a device used in microwave and radio frequency systems to split or combine power between two or more ports. Cavity couplers are mainly used in applications such as telecommunications, defense, aerospace, and medical.</p><p>The current outlook of the cavity coupler market is positive, with a steady growth rate expected over the forecasted period.
