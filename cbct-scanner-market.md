<p><p>A CBCT scanner, or Cone Beam Computed Tomography scanner, is a medical imaging device that uses cone-shaped X-ray beams and a two-dimensional detector to generate three-dimensional images of various body parts, including the teeth, jaws, and facial structures. It is commonly used in dental and maxillofacial imaging, as it provides detailed 3D images that aid in accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p><p>The CBCT scanner market has seen significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 12.1% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing prevalence of dental and maxillofacial conditions is driving the demand for advanced diagnostic tools like CBCT scanners. Additionally, the rising awareness among dental professionals regarding the benefits of CBCT scanners, such as reduced radiation exposure and improved treatment outcomes, is boosting the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements and the development of compact and portable CBCT scanners have further fueled market growth. These advancements have made CBCT scanners more accessible and affordable for clinics and small dental practices.</p><p>Furthermore, the integration of CBCT scanners with computer-aided design and computer-aided manufacturing (CAD/CAM) systems has enhanced their utility in dental procedures like implant placements and orthodontic treatments. This integration has contributed to the growing adoption of CBCT scanners in dental clinics.</p><p>In conclusion, the CBCT scanner market is experiencing steady growth due to factors such as the increasing prevalence of dental conditions, rising awareness among dental professionals, technological advancements, and the integration of CBCT scanners with CAD/CAM systems. The market is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 12.1% during the forecast period.</p></p>
