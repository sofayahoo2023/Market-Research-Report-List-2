<p><p>The cement enhancer market is divided into various types. Firstly, the air entraining agent is used to create small air bubbles in concrete, which improves its durability and resistance to freeze-thaw cycles. Secondly, the rust inhibitor prevents corrosion of reinforcing steel in concrete, thus maintaining its strength and integrity over time. Thirdly, the early strength agent accelerates the setting and hardening of cement, allowing for faster construction. Lastly, other types of cement enhancers include water reducing agents, superplasticizers, and shrinkage reducers, which enhance workability, reduce the water-cement ratio, and prevent cracking, respectively.</p></p>
