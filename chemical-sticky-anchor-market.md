<p><strong>The Chemical Sticky Anchor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injectable Adhensive Anchors</li><li>Capsule Adhensive Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemical Sticky Anchor market includes two main types: Injectable Adhesive Anchors and Capsule Adhesive Anchors. Injectable Adhesive Anchors involve using a special adhesive material that is injected into pre-drilled holes to form a strong bond with the surrounding substrate. This type is commonly used in construction, providing permanent anchorage for structural elements. Capsule Adhesive Anchors, on the other hand, use a two-component adhesive system contained in a single-use capsule. These anchors are designed for applications that require high load-bearing capacities, such as heavy machinery or seismic reinforcement.</p></p>
