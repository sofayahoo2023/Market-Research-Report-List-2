<p><p>Cleanroom ovens are classified into three types based on their capacity: small, standard, and large. Small capacity ovens have a volume of fewer than 2 cubic feet and are suitable for compact cleanroom environments. Standard capacity ovens range from 2 to 6 cubic feet and are commonly used in moderate-sized cleanrooms. Large capacity ovens, on the other hand, have a volume above 6 cubic feet and are ideal for spacious cleanroom facilities. These categorizations allow users to select the appropriate oven size according to their cleanroom requirements.</p></p>
