<p><p>Closed loop scrubbers are environmental devices used in industries to reduce air pollution and control harmful emissions. They are installed in exhaust systems of ships and power plants to remove sulfur oxides (SOx) from the exhaust gases before releasing them into the atmosphere. These scrubbers use a closed-loop system, in which water is used as a medium to absorb and neutralize the pollutants. The water is then treated and reused, resulting in minimal waste generation.</p><p>The current outlook of the closed loop scrubbers market is positive due to the increasing regulations on emissions from maritime and industrial sectors. The IMO (International Maritime Organization) has set strict SOx emission limits, which are driving the demand for scrubbers. Additionally, the growing concerns about air pollution and environmental sustainability are further boosting the market.</p><p>In terms of market growth analysis, the closed loop scrubbers market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
