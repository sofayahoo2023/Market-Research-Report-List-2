<p><ul><li>Milling Copra</li><li>Edible Copra</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coconut meat is classified into two main market types: Milling Copra and Edible Copra. Milling Copra refers to the meat of mature coconuts that are dried to a low moisture level, usually below 6%. It is commonly used for oil extraction and in the manufacturing of coconut-based products like soaps and cosmetics. On the other hand, Edible Copra refers to the meat of fresh coconuts that are harvested and consumed directly. It is highly valued for its taste and used in various culinary preparations.</p></p>
