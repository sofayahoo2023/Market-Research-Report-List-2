<p><p>The Collision Mitigation System market consists of four types: Radar, Camera, Ultrasound, and LiDAR. Radar-based systems use radio waves to detect objects and calculate their distance and speed. Camera-based systems utilize image sensors to identify potential collisions by analyzing visual data. Ultrasound-based systems utilize sound wave reflections to detect objects and estimate their distance. LiDAR-based systems employ laser beams to create detailed 3D maps and detect objects in their path. All these systems aim to prevent or minimize collisions and enhance overall vehicle safety.</p></p>
