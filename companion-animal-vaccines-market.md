<p><strong>The Companion Animal Vaccines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Attenuated Live Vaccines</li><li>Conjugate Vaccines</li><li>Inactivated Vaccines</li><li>Subunit Vaccines</li><li>Toxoid Vaccines</li><li>DNA Vaccines</li><li>Recombinant Vaccines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Companion animal vaccines play an essential role in preventing and controlling infectious diseases in pets. Attenuated live vaccines contain weakened forms of pathogens, stimulating an immune response without causing disease. Conjugate vaccines combine pathogen proteins with a carrier molecule, enhancing the immune response. Inactivated vaccines consist of killed pathogens, while subunit vaccines contain specific pathogen components. Toxoid vaccines use inactivated toxins, and DNA vaccines introduce genetic material to stimulate immunity. Recombinant vaccines are developed by inserting pathogen genes into a vector, triggering the production of desired antigens.</p></p>
