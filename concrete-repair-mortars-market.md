<p><p>Concrete Repair Mortars are used to repair and restore damaged or deteriorated concrete structures. The market is categorized into two types: Polymer-modified Cementitious Mortar and Epoxy-based Mortar. Polymer-modified Cementitious Mortars are made by adding polymers to cement, improving their flexibility, adhesion, and resistance to chemicals. They are commonly used for repairs in thin layers or for substrate preparation. Epoxy-based Mortars consist of epoxy resins and curing agents, providing high strength and durability. These mortars are ideal for structural repairs, bonding, and filling cracks and voids in concrete structures.</p></p>
