<p><p>Connected TV, also known as Smart TV or Hybrid TV, refers to television sets equipped with internet connectivity and built-in applications, allowing users to access digital content beyond traditional broadcast channels. Additionally, they offer features like web browsing, social networking, and home networking capabilities.</p><p>The Connected TV Market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period. Many smart TVs now come with voice-enabled remote controls or built-in voice assistants, allowing users to control the TV and access content using voice commands.</p><p>Another trend is the emergence of Over-The-Top (OTT) advertising on Connected TVs. As more users shift their viewing habits towards streaming platforms, advertisers are targeting these platforms to reach their target audience. OTT advertising offers targeted and personalized advertising, enabling advertisers to deliver more relevant content to viewers.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing the development of advanced features, such as 4K and HDR picture quality, to enhance the viewing experience. Manufacturers are also focusing on developing TVs with larger screens to cater to the increasing demand for home theater setups.</p><p>Overall, the Connected TV market is expected to witness significant growth over the coming years, driven by technological advancements, consumer preferences, and increasing demand for online streaming services.</p></p>
