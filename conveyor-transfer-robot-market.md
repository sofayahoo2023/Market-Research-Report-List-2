<p><p>The conveyor transfer robot market is segmented into two types: roller and belt. Roller conveyors are equipped with rotating tubes that facilitate the movement of items across a predefined path, allowing for easy transportation and efficient sorting. On the other hand, belt conveyors employ a continuous looped belt to transfer goods from one location to another, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Both roller and belt conveyors play a vital role in enhancing automation and productivity in industries such as manufacturing, warehousing, and logistics.</p></p>
