<p><ul><li>Sphygmomanometer</li><li>Automated Blood Pressure Monitor</li><li>BP Transducer</li><li>Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coronary pressure monitors are medical devices utilized for measuring blood pressure in the coronary arteries. Sphygmomanometers are manual devices consisting of a cuff, pressure gauge, and stethoscope. Automated blood pressure monitors are electronic devices that automatically measure blood pressure. BP transducers convert the pressure into an electrical signal for monitoring. Ambulatory blood pressure monitors are portable devices worn by patients to track blood pressure over a 24-hour period. These devices aid in diagnosing and managing cardiovascular conditions by providing accurate and continuous blood pressure measurements.</p></p>
