HDPE is a type of PEX that has a higher density and strength, making it suitable for applications requiring durability and resistance to high pressure and temperature. LDPE, on the other hand, has a lower density and is more flexible, making it ideal for applications requiring flexibility and ease of installation. Both HDPE and LDPE play important roles in various industries such as plumbing, heating, and cooling systems.</p></p>
