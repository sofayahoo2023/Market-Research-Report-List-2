<p><strong>The Data Center Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Interactive</li><li>Standby On-Line Hybrid</li><li>Double Conversion On-Line</li><li>Delta Conversion On-Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Center Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) System market consists of different types of UPS systems. Line Interactive UPS systems provide basic protection against power fluctuations and offer limited power backup. Standby On-Line Hybrid UPS systems provide medium-level protection by switching to battery power during outages. Double Conversion On-Line UPS systems offer complete protection by converting AC power to DC and then back to AC power. Delta Conversion On-Line UPS systems provide the highest level of protection with advanced technology that reduces power disturbance and increases efficiency.</p></p>
