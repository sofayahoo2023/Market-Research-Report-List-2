<p><p>DC response accelerometers are sensors used to measure acceleration in various applications. They are available in different market types based on the number of axis they can measure. 1-axis accelerometers measure acceleration along a single axis, typically in a linear or rotational motion. 2-axis accelerometers measure acceleration in two perpendicular axes, allowing for more comprehensive measurements. 3-axis accelerometers can measure acceleration in three orthogonal axes, providing a complete picture of an object's motion in three-dimensional space. These market types cater to different application needs, ranging from simple 1-axis measurements to more complex 3-axis measurements.</p></p>
