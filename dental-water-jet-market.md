<p><p>Dental Water Jet market consists of two primary types - Countertop Oral Irrigators and Cordless Oral Irrigators. Countertop Oral Irrigators are larger, stationary devices that require a power source and are typically used in a bathroom setting. These devices provide a powerful stream of water that helps remove plaque and food particles between teeth. On the other hand, Cordless Oral Irrigators are smaller, portable devices that run on rechargeable batteries. They offer the convenience of use anywhere and anytime, making them ideal for travel or on-the-go oral care routines. Both types of oral irrigators aim to improve oral hygiene and gum health.</p></p>
