<p><p>Descalers are products used to remove mineral deposits, limescale, and stains from various surfaces and appliances. They come in three main types: gel, liquid, and powder. Gel descalers have a thick consistency and are ideal for vertical surfaces or areas that require longer contact time. Liquid descalers are popular for general use and can be easily sprayed or poured onto surfaces. Powder descalers are highly concentrated and are typically mixed with water before application. Ultimately, the choice between these types depends on the specific needs and preferences of the user.</p></p>
