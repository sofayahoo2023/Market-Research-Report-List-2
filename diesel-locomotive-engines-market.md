<p><p>The diesel locomotive engines market comprises various types of engines used in locomotives. These include diesel-mechanical engines, which directly transmit power to the wheels through mechanical components; diesel-electric engines, where a generator produces electricity that powers electric motors connected to the wheels; diesel-hydraulic engines, which drive the wheels using hydraulic systems; diesel-steam engines, which use steam-pressure created by diesel heating to propel the locomotive; and diesel-pneumatic engines, which utilize compressed air to power pneumatic motors connected to the wheels.</p></p>
