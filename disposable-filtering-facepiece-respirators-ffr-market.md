<p><p>Disposable Filtering Facepiece Respirators (FFR) are respiratory protective devices designed to filter out harmful particles from the air. They are categorized into three market types based on their resistance to oil aerosols. N Class FFRs are not resistant to oil and provide protection against non-oil-based particles. P Class FFRs are oil-proof and provide protection against both oil and non-oil-based particles. R Class FFRs are oil-resistant but provide protection against non-oil-based particles only. These market types help categorize and specify the suitability of FFRs for different workplace environments and hazards.</p></p>
