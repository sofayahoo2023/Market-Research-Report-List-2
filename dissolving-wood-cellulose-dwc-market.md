<p><p>The Dissolving Wood Cellulose (DWC) market comprises two main types: Alkali Wood Pulp and Sulfate Wood Pulp. Alkali Wood Pulp is produced using a chemical process that involves treating wood chips with alkali, resulting in dissolving cellulose. Sulfate Wood Pulp, on the other hand, is obtained by digesting wood chips with sulfate, leading to the extraction of cellulose. Additionally, there is also a market for Wood Pulp of Sulfite, which involves dissolving cellulose from wood chips treated with sulfite.</p></p>
