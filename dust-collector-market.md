<p><p>The dust collector market consists of various types of dust collectors designed to remove dust and particles from industrial air streams. Bag dust collectors are the most common type, using fabric bags to capture dust particles. Electrostatic precipitators (ESP) use an electrostatic charge to attract and collect dust. Electrostatic-bag precipitators (EBP) combine both bag and ESP technologies for enhanced dust collection. Other types include cyclone separators, cartridge collectors, and wet scrubbers, each offering unique features and applications in the control of industrial dust emissions.</p></p>
