<p><p>The Electric Valve Positioner Market Types comprise two categories: Single Acting Positioners and Double Acting Positioners. Single Acting Positioners are designed to provide control over valve opening or closing in one direction, typically utilizing an air pressure signal to position the valve. On the other hand, Double Acting Positioners allow control over valve movement in both directions, using a combination of air pressure signals to accurately position the valve. These market types cater to different applications and offer varying degrees of control and functionality in valve positioning.</p></p>
