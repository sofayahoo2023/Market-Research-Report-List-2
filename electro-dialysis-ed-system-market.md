<p><p>The Electro Dialysis (ED) System Market consists of two main types: Continuous Electro Dialysis and Batch Electro Dialysis. Continuous Electro Dialysis involves the continuous separation of ions from a liquid mixture using an ion-selective membrane, allowing for a continuous flow of purified solution. Batch Electro Dialysis, on the other hand, operates in a discontinuous manner, separating ions in batches by passing them through an ion-exchange membrane. Both methods have their own unique advantages and applications, providing efficient solutions for purification and separation processes in various industries.</p></p>
