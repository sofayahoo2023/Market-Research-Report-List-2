<p><p>Electronic Beam Machining (EBM) is a precision machining method that utilizes a focused electron beam to remove material from a workpiece. EBM market types can be categorized into three main types: cathode, annular bias grid, and others. </p><p>In cathode EBM, a tungsten filament acts as the cathode and emits a beam of electrons towards the workpiece. Annular bias grid EBM uses an annular-shaped grid surrounding the cathode to enhance the focusing of the electron beam onto the workpiece. Lastly, other EBM market types can include variations and combinations of different electron beam generation and focusing techniques. Each market type offers unique advantages and applications in the field of electronic beam machining.</p></p>
