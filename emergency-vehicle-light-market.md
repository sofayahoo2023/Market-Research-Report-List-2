<p><p>The emergency vehicle light market is comprised of three main types: incandescent, halogen, and LED lights. Incandescent lights produce light by heating a filament within the bulb, but they are inefficient and have a short lifespan. Halogen lights are an improvement over incandescent lights as they use a halogen gas to extend the lifespan and brightness. However, LED lights are the most advanced option in the market. They offer superior energy efficiency, long lifespan, durability, and brighter light output, making them the preferred choice for emergency vehicle lighting.</p></p>
