It helps in bonding the solar cells to the glass and backsheet, providing electrical insulation. On the other hand, POE film, or polyolefin elastomer, is gaining popularity due to its improved weather resistance and resistance to yellowing over time. Other encapsulant materials include PVB, TPU, and silicone, which find niche applications in specific circumstances.</p></p>
