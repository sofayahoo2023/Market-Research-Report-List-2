<p><p>Enterprise Project Management Software (EPMS) can be categorized into two types: Cloud Based and On-Premise. Cloud-based EPMS refers to software solutions that are hosted and accessed over the internet by users. It offers flexibility, scalability, and convenience as it does not require infrastructure maintenance. On the other hand,On-Premise EPMS is installed on the organization's own servers and is managed by the internal IT team. This type provides more control over data security and customization options but requires more resources for maintenance and infrastructure.</p></p>
