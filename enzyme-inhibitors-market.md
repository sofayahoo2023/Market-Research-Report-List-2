<p><p>Enzyme inhibitors are substances that hinder the activity of specific enzymes in the body. Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs) reduce stomach acid secretion and are commonly used to treat conditions like acid reflux. Protease Inhibitors block the activity of protease enzymes and are employed in HIV treatment. Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors inhibit the replication of the HIV virus. Aromatase Inhibitors are used in breast cancer treatment. Kinase Inhibitors suppress the activity of kinases, which are involved in regulating cell growth. Neuraminidase Inhibitors are used to treat influenza. Statins decrease cholesterol production and help manage cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>
