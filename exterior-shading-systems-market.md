<p><p>Exterior shading systems refer to the installation of shading devices on the outside of buildings to regulate and control the amount of sunlight and heat entering the interior spaces. Two common types of installations are vertical and horizontal. Vertical installation involves mounting shading systems, such as blinds or screens, vertically along the windows or façade of a building. Horizontal installation, on the other hand, includes shading devices like awnings or canopies that are installed horizontally above windows or outdoor areas, providing effective shade and protection from direct sunlight.</p></p>
