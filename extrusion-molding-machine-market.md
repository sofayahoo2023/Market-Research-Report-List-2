<p><p>Extrusion molding machines are used to shape plastic or metal materials into specific forms. There are three types of these machines: hydraulic, all-electric, and hybrid. Hydraulic machines use hydraulic power to generate force and perform molding tasks. All-electric machines rely solely on electric power, resulting in higher precision and efficiency. Hybrid machines combine hydraulic and electric power, offering a balance between cost-effectiveness and performance. Overall, these machines cater to different production needs based on their power source and capabilities.</p></p>
