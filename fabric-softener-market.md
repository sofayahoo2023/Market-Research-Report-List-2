<p><p>Fabric softeners are products used in laundry to improve softness and static cling of clothing. There are two main types of fabric softeners available in the market: liquid fabric softener and fabric softener sheets. Liquid fabric softener is a liquid form that is added to the washing machine during the rinse cycle. It coats the fabric fibers, leaving clothes feeling softer and reducing static. Fabric softener sheets are pre-moistened sheets that are added to the dryer with the clothes. As they heat up, the sheets release a fabric softener that coats the clothes, creating a soft and static-free finish.</p></p>
