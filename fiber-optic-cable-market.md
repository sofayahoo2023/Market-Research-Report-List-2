<p><p>The fiber optic cable market is comprised of two main types: single-mode and multi-mode fiber optic cables. Single-mode cables have a thin core which allows only one mode of light to propagate, enabling longer transmission distances and higher bandwidth. On the other hand, multi-mode cables have a larger core which allows multiple modes of light to propagate, resulting in shorter transmission distances and lower bandwidth. Both types have their own unique applications and are used extensively in various industries for efficient data transmission.</p></p>
