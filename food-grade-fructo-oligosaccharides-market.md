<p><p>Food Grade Fructo Oligosaccharides (FOS) are available in two main forms in the market: powder and syrup. Powdered FOS is a dry and fine substance made by evaporating the liquid FOS solution, resulting in a concentrated form that can be easily incorporated into various food products. FOS syrup, on the other hand, is a thick and viscous liquid obtained through a different manufacturing process, offering a convenient ready-to-use option for applications such as beverages and confectionery. Both powder and syrup forms of FOS provide the same nutritional benefits and are widely used in the food industry.</p></p>
