<p><p>The Form-in-Place (FIP) liquid gaskets market encompasses two main types: conductive FIP gaskets and non-conductive FIP gaskets. Conductive FIP gaskets are designed to provide electrical conductivity, allowing for the flow of electricity between two surfaces. Non-conductive FIP gaskets, on the other hand, are intended to insulate and prevent electrical conductivity between surfaces. Both types of FIP gaskets are used in various industries and applications to ensure proper sealing, protection, and performance of electronic components and devices.</p></p>
