<p><p>The fully automatic urine analyzer market can be classified into two types - microscopy method and chemistry method. The microscopy method involves the use of a microscope to observe and analyze urine samples for the presence of abnormalities such as bacteria or red and white blood cells. On the other hand, the chemistry method uses chemical reagents to measure and analyze various parameters in urine, including glucose, protein, and pH levels. Both methods provide accurate results and are widely used in medical laboratories to diagnose and monitor urinary tract conditions and diseases.</p></p>
