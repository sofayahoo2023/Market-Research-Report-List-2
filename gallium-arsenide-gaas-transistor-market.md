<p><p>The Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) Transistor market covers several types of transistors. Field Effect Transistor (FET) is a popular type that regulates the flow of current using an electric field. Pseudomorphic High Electron Mobility Transistor (pHEMT) ensures high-speed switching and low noise. Metal-Semiconductor Field Effect Transistor (MESFET) relies on metal-semiconductor junctions for current control. High Electron Mobility Transistor (HEMT) provides high-frequency operation and low noise characteristics. Enhancement-mode pHEMT (E-pHEMT) is suitable for power amplifiers. Lastly, Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field Effect Transistor (MOSFET) ensures efficient power handling and precise control of current flow.</p></p>
