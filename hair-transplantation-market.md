<p><ul><li>Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT)</li><li>Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hair Transplantation is a booming market that offers different techniques to restore hair growth. Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) involves removing a strip of scalp from the donor area and then dissecting it into individual follicular units for transplantation. Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) requires extracting individual hair follicles directly from the donor area using specialized instruments. Both FUT and FUE are common procedures in hair restoration, offering patients options to combat hair loss and achieve natural-looking results.</p></p>
