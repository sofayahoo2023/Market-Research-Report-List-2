<p><p>Healthcare payment processing market can be classified into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premise payment processing involves hosting the payment solution on local servers, allowing providers to have complete control over their data and security. While cloud-based solutions are more popular due to their convenience, some providers prefer on-premise solutions for enhanced control and data protection.</p></p>
