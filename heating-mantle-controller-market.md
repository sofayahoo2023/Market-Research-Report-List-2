<p><p>The Heating Mantle Controller market can be categorized into two types: Double Circuit and Single Circuit. A Double Circuit Controller consists of two separate circuits that allow independent temperature control of two heating mantles. This type of controller is suitable for applications where different temperature requirements are needed simultaneously. On the other hand, a Single Circuit Controller has only one circuit and can control the temperature of a single heating mantle. This type of controller is suitable for applications where only one temperature control is required.</p></p>
