<p><p>There are two main types of high chairs in the market: traditional high chairs and booster seats. Traditional high chairs are standalone chairs with a raised seat, often equipped with a tray or food tray, and safety harness to secure the child. They are usually used from infancy until the child can sit independently. On the other hand, booster seats are designed to be placed on top of a regular chair, elevating the child to the table's height. They are typically used for older babies and toddlers who can sit unassisted but still require some support.</p></p>
