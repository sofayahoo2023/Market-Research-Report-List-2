<p><p>Hot air plastic welders are commonly categorized into two types: manual and automatic. Manual hot air plastic welders require the operator to control the welding process by manually manipulating the welder's temperature, speed, and pressure. They are suitable for small-scale projects and offer more control and flexibility. On the other hand, automatic hot air plastic welders have automated controls for temperature, speed, and pressure, eliminating the need for manual adjustments. These are ideal for large-scale or continuous welding applications, offering ease of use and higher productivity.</p></p>
