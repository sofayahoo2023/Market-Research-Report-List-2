<p><p>Hotmelt TPU film is a versatile material used in various industries. It can be classified into three market types based on its breathability level. Low Breathable TPU Film is designed to have minimal breathability, making it suitable for applications requiring high barrier properties. Medium Breathable TPU Film offers a moderate level of breathability, balancing barrier properties with moisture vapor transmission. High Breathable TPU Film provides excellent breathability, allowing moisture vapor to pass through easily. These market types cater to different requirements and find applications in sectors like textiles, medical, and packaging.</p></p>
