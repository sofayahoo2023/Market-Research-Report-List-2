<p><strong>The HR Document Management Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-Based</li><li>On-Premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HR Document Management Software includes two main market types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise solutions. Cloud-Based software is hosted on the Internet, allowing users to access documents and tools remotely. It eliminates the need for hardware and software maintenance, offering scalability and accessibility on various devices. On the other hand, On-Premise software is installed locally on a company's servers. It puts the responsibility of maintenance and security on the organization but ensures complete control over data and infrastructure. Both options are designed to streamline HR operations and enhance document management efficiency.</p></p>
