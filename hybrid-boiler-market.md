<p><p>Hybrid boilers are a type of heating system that combines the use of a conventional boiler with a heat pump. There are different types of hybrid boilers available in the market. </p><p>One type is the boiler with an air source heat pump, which extracts heat from the outside air and uses it to supplement the boiler's heating capabilities. This helps improve efficiency and reduce energy consumption.</p><p>Another type is the boiler with a ground source heat pump, which utilizes heat from the ground through a series of pipes buried underground. This provides an eco-friendly and efficient heating solution.</p><p>Lastly, the "Others" category includes hybrid boilers that may use alternative heat sources, such as solar energy or waste heat recovery systems, to further optimize energy efficiency and reduce carbon emissions.</p></p>
