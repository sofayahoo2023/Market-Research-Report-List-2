<p><p>Hydraulic motor market is classified into various types, including vane type, plunger type, and others. Vane type hydraulic motors utilize vanes inserted inside a rotor, which rotate due to the flow of hydraulic fluid. Plunger type hydraulic motors comprise a series of plungers that move back and forth, converting hydraulic energy into mechanical rotation. Other types may include gear motors, piston motors, or screw motors, among others. Each type of hydraulic motor offers unique features and applications, catering to the diverse needs of industries such as construction, agriculture, and manufacturing.</p></p>
