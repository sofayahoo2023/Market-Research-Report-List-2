<p><p>Hydraulic piston pumps are categorized into two main types: Multiple Displacements and Single Displacement. </p><p>Multiple Displacements pumps offer various displacement options, allowing for flexible flow control. These pumps have different cylinder sizes, resulting in varying flow rates and pressures as required by different applications.</p><p>On the other hand, Single Displacement pumps have a fixed displacement volume. They provide a constant flow rate and pressure and are widely used in applications that demand a consistent hydraulic power output.</p></p>
