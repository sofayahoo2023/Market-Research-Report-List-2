Fiberglass insulation is made from glass fibers and is commonly used for thermal insulation in pipelines. Mineral wool, made from mineral fibers, is known for its high-temperature resistance. Elastomeric insulation consists of cellular rubber and is suitable for both high and low-temperature applications. PE insulation, made from polyethylene, is widely used due to its cost-effectiveness and good thermal performance. These insulation materials provide effective energy conservation, temperature control, and condensation prevention for industrial piping systems.</p></p>
