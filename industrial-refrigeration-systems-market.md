Condensers are responsible for cooling and condensing the refrigerant by releasing the heat absorbed during the process. Evaporators facilitate the refrigerant's evaporation, which absorbs heat from the surroundings to cause cooling. These components collectively ensure efficient and reliable refrigeration, allowing industries to maintain the freshness and quality of their perishable products.</p></p>
