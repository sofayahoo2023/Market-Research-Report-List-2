<p><p>The Insulin Management System market can be categorized into three types: standalone systems, sensor-augmented pump systems, and closed-loop systems. Standalone systems consist of insulin pens and insulin pumps, which help individuals manually administer insulin doses. Sensor-augmented pump systems combine insulin pumps with continuous glucose monitoring systems to provide real-time glucose readings and automated insulin delivery. Closed-loop systems, often referred to as artificial pancreas systems, utilize advanced algorithms to automatically adjust insulin delivery based on glucose levels, offering a more integrated and automated approach to insulin management.</p></p>
