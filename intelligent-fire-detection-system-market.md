<p><p>The Intelligent Fire Detection System market consists of two main types: Addressable Fire Detection Systems and Conventional Fire Detection Systems. Addressable Fire Detection Systems enable the user to identify the exact location of fire incidents through individual device addresses, providing real-time data and precise information for faster response. On the other hand, Conventional Fire Detection Systems divide buildings into zones and indicate the presence of a fire without specifying the exact location. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring fire safety, with addressable systems offering enhanced accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
