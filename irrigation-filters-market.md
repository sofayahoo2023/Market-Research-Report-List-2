<p><p>The irrigation filters market consists of two main types: automatic and semi-automatic filters. Automatic filters are designed to operate independently and require minimal human intervention. They use advanced technology to automatically clean and maintain the filtration system, ensuring efficient water flow and preventing clogging. On the other hand, semi-automatic filters require occasional manual intervention to clean the filters. Although they offer similar benefits as automatic filters, they require more effort and attention from the user. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing irrigation systems and ensuring high-quality water supply for agricultural and landscaping purposes.</p></p>
