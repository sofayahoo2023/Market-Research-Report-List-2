<p><p>ISO 5199 Pumps is a standard that specifies the requirements for centrifugal pumps used in process industries. Within this market, there are two main types: Magdrive Pumps and Sealed Pumps. Magdrive Pumps utilize a magnetic coupling to transmit power, eliminating the need for a traditional mechanical seal. This reduces the risk of leakage and improves safety. On the other hand, Sealed Pumps use mechanical seals to prevent leakage. Both types cater to different industry needs, ensuring efficient and reliable pumping solutions.</p></p>
