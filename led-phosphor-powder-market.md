<p><p>The LED phosphor powder market includes different types of phosphor powders used in LED lights. Red phosphor powder is a type of phosphor that emits red light when excited by a blue LED. It is commonly used in displays and indicator lights. Yellow phosphor powder is another type of phosphor that emits yellow light when excited by a near-ultraviolet LED. This type of phosphor is used in applications like general lighting and backlighting for LCD displays. Both red and yellow phosphor powders contribute to the color rendering capabilities of LED lights.</p></p>
