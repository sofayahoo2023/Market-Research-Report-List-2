<p><p>The LED stage curtains market can be classified into two types: monolithic and stitching. Monolithic curtains are made from a single piece of LED fabric, providing a seamless and continuous display surface. On the other hand, stitching curtains are constructed by joining multiple LED panels together, creating a larger display area through the stitching process. Both types offer unique advantages and can be tailored to suit different stage requirements, providing enhanced visual effects and versatile options for stage designers.</p></p>
