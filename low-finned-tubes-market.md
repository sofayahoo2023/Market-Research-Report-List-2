<p><p>Low finned tubes are heat exchanger tubes with enhanced heat transfer surfaces. There are two main types of low finned tubes in the market:</p><p>1. Single Metal Low Finned Tubes: These tubes have fins formed from the same metal as the tube itself. The fins are either extruded or welded onto the base tube, resulting in increased heat transfer efficiency and improved performance.</p><p>2. Poly Metal Low Finned Tubes: In contrast, poly metal low finned tubes have fins made from a different metal alloy compared to the base tube. This combination of metals provides enhanced corrosion resistance and ensures better performance in harsh operating conditions.</p></p>
