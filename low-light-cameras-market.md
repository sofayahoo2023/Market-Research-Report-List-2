<p><p>The Low Light Cameras market includes different types such as Active IR Focus, CCD AF, and Phase Difference Measurement Focus. Active IR Focus cameras use infrared light to detect and focus on subjects in low light conditions. CCD AF cameras employ a charge-coupled device to focus accurately on subjects. Lastly, Phase Difference Measurement Focus cameras use phase difference detection to quickly and accurately focus on subjects. These different types of low light cameras offer various methods to enhance focusing capabilities in challenging lighting environments.</p></p>
