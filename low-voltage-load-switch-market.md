<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The low voltage load switch market consists of two main types: oil-immersed type and vacuum type. Oil-immersed load switches are designed to operate by submerging the contacts in oil, which provides insulation and acts as an arc extinguisher. Vacuum load switches, on the other hand, utilize vacuum technology to interrupt the electrical current. Both types are used in low voltage applications, with oil-immersed switches being more commonly used in indoor installations and vacuum switches often preferred for outdoor or harsh environments.</p></p>
