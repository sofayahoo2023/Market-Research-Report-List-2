RTE meals are fully prepared and require no additional cooking, offering convenience to consumers. RTH meals are partially cooked and need heating before consumption, providing a quick and hassle-free option. RTC meals offer raw ingredients along with detailed instructions, allowing consumers to cook the meal themselves. These market types cater to different consumer needs and preferences, offering a range of convenient meal options.</p></p>
