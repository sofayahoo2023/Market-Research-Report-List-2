<p><p>Medical assistive devices refer to a wide range of products, instruments, or tools that aid individuals with disabilities or limitations in performing daily activities, improving their overall quality of life. Firstly, the rising prevalence of chronic diseases, such as cardiovascular disorders, respiratory diseases, and age-related conditions, has increased the demand for assistive devices to aid patient care and rehabilitation.
