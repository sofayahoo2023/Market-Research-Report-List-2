<p><p>Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) systems are utilized in wastewater treatment processes to improve the efficiency of biological and filtration processes. There are two main types of MBR systems: Separate MBR and Integral MBR. </p><p>Separate MBR systems consist of separate bioreactors and membrane modules, which are connected via a filtration unit. This allows for flexibility in system design and operation, as well as easy maintenance and cleaning of individual components. </p><p>In contrast, Integral MBR systems integrate the bioreactor and membrane modules into a single unit. This compact design offers advantages in terms of reduced footprint and simplified installation and operation.</p></p>
