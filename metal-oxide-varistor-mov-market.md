<p><p>A Metal Oxide Varistor (MOV) is a type of voltage-dependent resistor that is primarily used for surge protection in electronic devices and power distribution systems. It consists of a ceramic disk that contains a metal oxide, typically zinc oxide. MOVs have the ability to conduct electricity when a low voltage is applied, but when a high voltage surge occurs, they become highly resistive, thereby protecting the connected devices from damage.</p><p>The Metal Oxide Varistor (MOV) Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for MOVs can be attributed to the rising need for surge protection devices in various industries, such as automotive, electronics, and telecommunications. The growing adoption of electronic devices coupled with the increasing awareness regarding the protection of sensitive electronic components against power surges is driving the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology and the continuous development of new MOV products are further fueling market growth.
