<p><p>Micro screwdrivers are small-sized screwdrivers specifically designed for working with miniature screws. They come in different market types, including flat micro screwdrivers, Phillips head micro screwdrivers, Allen wrench micro screwdrivers, and others. Flat micro screwdrivers have a flat tip, which is suitable for screws with a single slot. Phillips head micro screwdrivers have a cross-shaped tip that fits into screws with a corresponding cross-shaped slot. Allen wrench micro screwdrivers have a hexagonal-shaped tip and are used for screws with a hexagonal socket. The "others" category includes micro screwdrivers with different specialized tips for specific screw types or applications.</p></p>
