<p><strong>The Microneedles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hollow Microneedle Technology</li><li>Solid Microneedle Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microneedles market comprises two key types: hollow microneedle technology and solid microneedle technology. Hollow microneedles have a small hollow channel in their center, allowing for the delivery or extraction of fluids or substances. This technology is commonly used for drug delivery applications, including vaccines and cosmetic formulations. Conversely, solid microneedles lack a hollow channel but are designed to penetrate the skin effectively, promoting the absorption of topically applied substances. Solid microneedle technology finds applications in areas such as skincare, pain management, and transdermal drug delivery.</p></p>
