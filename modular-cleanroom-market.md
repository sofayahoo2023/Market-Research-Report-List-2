<p><ul><li>Modular Softwall Cleanrooms</li><li>Modular Hardwall Cleanrooms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modular cleanrooms refer to controlled environments that are constructed using modular components. There are two main types of modular cleanrooms: modular softwall cleanrooms and modular hardwall cleanrooms. A modular softwall cleanroom utilizes flexible curtain-like walls made of materials like vinyl or PVC, making it easy to install, modify, and expand. On the other hand, a modular hardwall cleanroom employs rigid walls made of materials like aluminum or steel, offering enhanced durability and stability. Both types of modular cleanrooms are utilized in various industries to maintain controlled conditions for sensitive processes.</p></p>
