<p><p>The narrowband Internet of Things (IoT) chipset market can be categorized into three types: Stand-Alone, Guard Band, and In-Band. Stand-Alone refers to a dedicated frequency band exclusively for IoT devices, ensuring interference-free communication. Guard Band involves utilizing the space between two frequency bands to accommodate IoT devices. In-Band integrates IoT signals within the same frequency band as other wireless communication systems. These categories determine the approach taken while implementing IoT chipsets for connectivity, enabling reliable and efficient communication in various IoT applications.</p></p>
