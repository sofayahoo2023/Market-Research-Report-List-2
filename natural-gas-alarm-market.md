<p><ul><li>Stationary Gas Alarms</li><li>Portable Gas Alarms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural gas alarm market is divided into two main types: stationary gas alarms and portable gas alarms. Stationary gas alarms are fixed in a specific location, typically in households or commercial buildings, and are designed to detect any leaks of natural gas. They provide continuous monitoring and can be connected to an alarm system or shut off valves for immediate action. On the other hand, portable gas alarms are compact and can be carried around by individuals. They are useful for outdoor activities or in situations where gas leaks may occur temporarily.</p></p>
