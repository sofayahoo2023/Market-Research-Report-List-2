<p><p>Needle-free injectors are medical devices that enable the delivery of medication through the skin without the use of a traditional needle. There are two types of needle-free injectors: fillable and prefilled. Fillable needle-free injectors require manual filling by the healthcare professional, allowing for flexibility in the choice of medication and dosage. Prefilled needle-free injectors, on the other hand, come preloaded with a specific medication and dosage, ready for immediate administration. Both types offer the advantage of painless drug delivery, reduced needlestick injuries, and improved patient compliance.</p></p>
