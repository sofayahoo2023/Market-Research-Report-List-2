Single lumen needle knives have a single channel, allowing for a single instrument or wire to be passed through. Triple lumen needle knives have three separate channels, enabling the simultaneous passage of instruments or wires. Other market variations may include different numbers of lumens or additional features specific to certain medical procedures. These variations offer healthcare professionals the flexibility to choose a needle knife that best suits their specific requirements during endoscopic procedures.</p></p>
