<p><strong>The Non-Contact Seals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gap Seals</li><li>Labyrinth Seal</li><li>Gasket Seal</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-contact seals are sealing devices used in various industries to prevent the entry of contaminants or fluids into systems without physical contact. Gap seals create a minimal gap between surfaces, providing a barrier against unwanted particles. Labyrinth seals consist of multiple interlocking components that create a tortuous path, preventing leakage. Gasket seals utilize flat or profiled gaskets to create a seal between two surfaces. Other types of non-contact seals include brush seals, air seals, and magnetic seals, each with their unique features and applications in specific industries.</p></p>
