Silicon carbide ceramics are highly durable, heat-resistant, and corrosion-resistant, making them suitable for applications such as abrasive machining and refractory linings. On the other hand, silicon nitride ceramics exhibit excellent mechanical strength, thermal stability, and chemical resistance, making them ideal for use in aerospace, automotive, and medical industries. Both types of ceramics are widely utilized in high-performance applications due to their exceptional properties.</p></p>
