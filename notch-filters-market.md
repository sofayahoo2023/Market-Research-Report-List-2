<p><ul><li>Tunable</li><li>Non-Tunable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Notch filters are electronic devices used to attenuate or eliminate specific frequencies from a signal. Tunable notch filters are adjustable and can be fine-tuned to select a specific frequency range for filtering. They offer flexibility and are commonly used in applications where the frequency to be blocked or attenuated may vary. Non-tunable notch filters, on the other hand, have a fixed frequency range and cannot be adjusted. They are typically used in applications where the specific frequency to be filtered remains constant and does not require any adjustments.</p></p>
