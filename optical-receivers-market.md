<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Optical Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy</li><li>Synchronous Digital Hierarchy</li><li>Synchronous Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical receivers market comprises three significant types: Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy (PDH), Synchronous Digital Hierarchy (SDH), and Synchronous Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy (SPDH). PDH is an older, lower-capacity technology used for voice and data transmission. SDH is a more advanced and standardized technology that allows for higher transmission rates and greater flexibility. SPDH combines the features of both PDH and SDH, enabling synchronization and traffic management at different transmission rates. These market types cater to different needs in terms of capacity, synchronization, and flexibility within the optical communication network industry.</p></p>
