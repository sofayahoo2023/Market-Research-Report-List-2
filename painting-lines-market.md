<p><p>Fully Automatic Painting Lines are systems that require minimal human intervention and can carry out the entire painting process independently. These lines automate tasks such as surface preparation, painting, and drying, resulting in increased efficiency and productivity. On the other hand, Semi-Automatic Painting Lines involve partial automation of the painting process, with some tasks being manually performed by operators. These lines allow for greater flexibility and customization, as operators can make adjustments and carry out specific tasks that require human judgment and skills. Both market types cater to industries that require large-scale painting operations.</p></p>
