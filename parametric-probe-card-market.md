<p><ul><li>Low Leakage</li><li>Ultra Low Leakage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parametric probe cards are used in semiconductor testing to evaluate the electrical performance of integrated circuits. The market is divided into two types - low leakage and ultra-low leakage. Low leakage probe cards are designed to minimize current leakage during testing, ensuring accurate and reliable measurements. Ultra-low leakage probe cards take this a step further by significantly reducing current leakage to achieve even higher precision testing. Both types cater to different needs and requirements of semiconductor manufacturers, offering varying levels of accuracy in evaluating circuit performance.</p></p>
