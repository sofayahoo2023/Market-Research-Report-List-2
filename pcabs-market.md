<p><p>PC/ABS (Polycarbonate/Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) is a thermoplastic blend composed of polycarbonate and acrylonitrile butadiene styrene. PC/ABS is widely used in various applications such as automotive, electronics, appliances, and consumer goods.</p><p>The future outlook of the PC/ABS market appears promising. The increasing demand for lightweight and durable materials in the automotive industry to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions is expected to drive the market growth. PC/ABS is extensively used in automotive interior and exterior parts due to its high impact resistance and aesthetic appeal.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing electronics industry, driven by technological advancements and the rising adoption of smartphones, tablets, and wearable devices, is anticipated to contribute to the market expansion. PC/ABS is used in the production of electrical housings, connectors, and other components due to its excellent electrical insulation and flame retardant properties.</p><p>The current outlook of the PC/ABS market is positive. The increasing focus on sustainability and eco-friendly materials is driving the demand for PC/ABS as it can be recycled and offers good chemical resistance.
