<p><ul><li>Cold Chain Logistics</li><li>Non-cold Chain Logistics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pharmaceutical Logistics Market is divided into two main types: Cold Chain Logistics and Non-cold Chain Logistics. Cold Chain Logistics refers to the transportation and storage of temperature-sensitive pharmaceutical products that require controlled refrigerated conditions. This includes vaccines, biologics, and other perishable medicines. On the other hand, Non-cold Chain Logistics deals with the transportation and storage of pharmaceutical products that do not require refrigeration or specific temperature control. These are typically solid dosage forms like tablets, capsules, and powders. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the integrity and effectiveness of pharmaceutical products throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
