<p><ul><li>Positive Type Photoresist Stripper</li><li>Negative Type Photoresist Stripper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photoresist Stripper market consists of two main types: Positive Type and Negative Type. Positive Type Photoresist Stripper is used to remove the exposed areas of the photoresist material, leaving only the unexposed areas intact. On the other hand, Negative Type Photoresist Stripper is used to eliminate the unexposed areas, while preserving the exposed areas. These different types cater to specific applications where photoresist materials need to be selectively removed based on whether they were exposed or unexposed during the photolithography process.</p></p>
