<p><p>Photoresist stripping equipment refers to the machines used to remove photoresist materials from semiconductor wafers during the manufacturing process. Dry process equipment involves the use of gases or plasma to remove the photoresist, while wet process equipment uses chemicals and solvents for the same purpose. The dry process equipment offers faster and more precise stripping, while wet process equipment is more cost-effective but may require longer processing times.</p></p>
