<p><p>The PIR-based motion detector market can be categorized into three types: single element, dual element, and others. Single element motion detectors consist of one PIR sensor that detects infrared radiation changes caused by movement. Dual element motion detectors have two PIR sensors positioned in such a way that they can differentiate between false alarms and actual motion, improving accuracy. Other market types may include advanced motion detectors with additional features like pet immunity or long-range detection. These different types cater to varying customer needs, providing options in terms of sensitivity, accuracy, and additional functionalities.</p></p>
