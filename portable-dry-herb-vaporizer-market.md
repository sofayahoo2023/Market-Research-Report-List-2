<p><p>The portable dry herb vaporizer market offers two types: conduction and convection. Conduction vaporizers heat the herb by direct contact with a heating element. They are generally more affordable and have faster heat-up times. On the other hand, convection vaporizers heat the herb by passing hot air through it, resulting in a more even and efficient vaporization process. Convection vaporizers are usually more expensive but provide better flavor and vapor quality. Both types offer convenient and portable options for enjoying dry herb vaporization on the go.</p></p>
