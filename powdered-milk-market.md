<p><p>Powdered milk, also known as dried milk, is a dairy product made by evaporating milk to remove most of the water content. Powdered milk offers a convenient alternative to regular milk, as it has a longer shelf life and is easier to store and transport.</p><p>The powdered milk market has witnessed significant growth in recent years. The increasing demand for dairy products and the rising need for food preservation are the key drivers of this market. Powdered milk is widely used in various applications such as bakery, confectionery, infant formula, and ready-to-eat meals. The growing population, urbanization, and changing dietary preferences have led to an increased consumption of powdered milk across the globe.</p><p>Moreover, the convenience factor associated with powdered milk, especially in areas with limited access to fresh milk, has further boosted its demand.
