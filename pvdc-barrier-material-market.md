<p><p>The PVDC barrier material market comprises PVDC resins and PVDC latex as its two main types. PVDC resins are solid materials that provide exceptional barrier properties against moisture, oxygen, and aromas when used in various packaging applications. On the other hand, PVDC latex is a water-based dispersion that is primarily used in coating applications to enhance the barrier properties of paper and films. Both PVDC resins and PVDC latex play a crucial role in maintaining product freshness and shelf life by preventing the permeation of gases and moisture.</p></p>
