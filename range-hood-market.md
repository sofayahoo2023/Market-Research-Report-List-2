Different types of range hoods cater to various kitchen setups and needs. Under-cabinet hoods are mounted under the cabinet, saving space. Wall-chimney hoods are installed against the wall, perfect for kitchens with a cooktop against the wall. Island hoods are suspended from the ceiling, ideally suited for cooking islands. Downdraft hoods are integrated into the cooking surface, allowing for a sleek design. Other types of range hoods include ductless hoods, pro-style hoods, and insert liner hoods.</p></p>
