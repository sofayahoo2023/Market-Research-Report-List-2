<p><ul><li>Lateral Flow Test</li><li>Agglutination Assays</li><li>Flow Through</li><li>Solid Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rapid diagnostic kits market encompasses different types of testing methods. Lateral flow tests are simple and quick, revealing a positive or negative result. Agglutination assays detect specific antibodies or antigens by clumping together. Flow through tests operate by moving the sample through a membrane to detect specific analytes. Solid phase tests involve capturing biomarkers using a solid support. These methods are widely used in healthcare settings for their ease of use, quick results, and ability to diagnose various conditions effectively.</p></p>
