<p><p>Recon software market includes two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based recon software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access it over the internet. This type offers easier scalability, cost savings, and flexibility. On the other hand, on-premise software is installed and deployed locally on company servers. It provides greater control, security, and customization options but requires higher initial setup costs and IT expertise. Both market types aim to assist businesses in efficiently reconciling data, transactions, and accounts within their financial systems.</p></p>
