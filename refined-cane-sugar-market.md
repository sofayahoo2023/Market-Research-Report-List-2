<p><p>The refined cane sugar market encompasses three main types: liquid sugar, powdered sugar, and granulated sugar. Liquid sugar is a viscous syrup commonly used in beverages and processed foods. Powdered sugar, also known as confectioners' sugar, is finely ground sugar mixed with a small amount of cornstarch, mainly used in baking and icing. Lastly, granulated sugar is the most common type, consisting of sugar crystals of medium size often used for general cooking and sweetening. These different forms cater to specific culinary needs and preferences in various industries and households.</p></p>
