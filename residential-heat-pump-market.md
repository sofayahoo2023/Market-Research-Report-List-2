It is an energy-efficient alternative to traditional heating and cooling systems like electric heaters, furnaces, and air conditioners. Residential heat pumps have gained popularity due to their ability to reduce energy consumption and lower carbon emissions.</p><p>The future outlook of the residential heat pump market is promising.
