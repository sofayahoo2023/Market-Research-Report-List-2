<p><p>Retail Asset Protection refers to a set of techniques and strategies implemented by retailers to safeguard their assets from theft, fraud, and other risks. Retailers are leveraging technologies like IoT and cloud computing to connect various security systems and gain centralized control and monitoring capabilities. This integration allows real-time monitoring, proactive threat detection, and efficient incident response.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has also influenced the retail asset protection market. Retailers have been adopting contactless technologies, implementing stricter health and safety measures, and enhancing security to ensure compliance with social distancing guidelines and protect their assets during these challenging times.</p><p>In conclusion, the retail asset protection market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing need for loss prevention, advancements in technology, and changing retail landscape.
