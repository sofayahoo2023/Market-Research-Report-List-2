<p><p>RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) is a technology used for tracking and identifying objects using radio waves. In the industrial sector, RFID is utilized for various applications. The market for RFID in industrial applications can be divided into three types: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultra High Frequency (UHF). LF RFID operates at a low frequency range of 30 kHz to 300 kHz, HF RFID operates at a higher frequency range of 3.56 MHz, and UHF RFID operates at an even higher frequency range of 860 MHz to 960 MHz. Each type offers different advantages and is used based on specific requirements within industrial environments.</p></p>
