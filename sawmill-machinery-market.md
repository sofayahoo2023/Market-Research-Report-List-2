<p><p>The sawmill machinery market can be divided into two types: fixed sawmill machinery and portable sawmill machinery. Fixed sawmill machinery refers to equipment that is permanently installed and operates in a fixed location. It is commonly used by large-scale industrial sawmills. On the other hand, portable sawmill machinery is mobile and can be easily transported to different locations. This type of machinery is often used by small-scale or hobbyist sawmill operators who need flexibility and the ability to move their equipment to various job sites.</p></p>
