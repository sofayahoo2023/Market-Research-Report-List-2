PA 6T is a semiaromatic polyamide made from hexamethylene diamine and teraphthalic acid, offering excellent high-temperature resistance, flame retardancy, and mechanical properties. On the other hand, PA 6I is derived from hexamethylene diamine and isophthalic acid, providing high strength, chemical resistance, and dimensional stability.
