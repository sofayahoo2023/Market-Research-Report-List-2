<p><strong>The Semiconductor X-ray Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Detector</li><li>Indirect Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor X-ray detectors are used in various applications including healthcare, aerospace, and industrial sectors. Direct detectors directly convert X-rays into electrical signals, offering high efficiency, resolution, and speed. Indirect detectors use scintillator materials to convert X-rays into visible light, which is then detected by a semiconductor material. Indirect detectors offer a wider dynamic range and better temporal resolution compared to direct detectors. Both types play a crucial role in X-ray imaging technologies, providing accurate and efficient detection of X-ray radiation for different applications.</p></p>
