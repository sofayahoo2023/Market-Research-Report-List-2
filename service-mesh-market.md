As organizations increasingly shift towards cloud-native applications, the need to manage and secure the complex network of microservices has become crucial. Service mesh provides a comprehensive solution to address these challenges, leading to its growing popularity.</p><p>Additionally, the rising demand for containerization and orchestration technologies like Kubernetes has further propelled the market growth.
