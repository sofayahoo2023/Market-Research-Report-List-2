<p><p>Other azimuth thrusters and lateral thrusters are two types of ship thrusters used for maneuverability and propulsion in marine vessels. </p><p>Other azimuth thrusters, also known as omni-directional thrusters, are capable of rotating 360 degrees horizontally and offer enhanced maneuverability for precise positioning and easy navigation in narrow spaces. These thrusters are commonly used in dynamic positioning systems and offshore applications.</p><p>Lateral thrusters, on the other hand, provide lateral propulsion and are typically mounted on the sides of a ship. They enable lateral movement and assist in steering the vessel, especially during docking and berthing operations. Lateral thrusters enhance the ship's handling capabilities and ensure safe and efficient maneuvering in confined waters.</p></p>
