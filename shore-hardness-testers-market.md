<p><p>Shore hardness testers are used to measure the hardness of materials. There are two types of market for these testers: analog and digital. Analog shore hardness testers use a needle to measure the resistance of the material, which is then read on a scale. Digital shore hardness testers, on the other hand, display the hardness measurement directly on a digital screen. Both types have their advantages - analog testers are more cost-effective and simpler to operate, while digital testers offer more accurate and precise measurements.</p></p>
