<p><p>The Shortwave Infrared Equipment market includes two major types: Area Scan and Line Scan. In Area Scan, the equipment captures images from a stationary position, providing a broad picture across a specific area. This type is commonly used in security surveillance systems. On the other hand, Line Scan involves capturing images by moving the equipment along a linear path. This type is preferred for high-speed applications like industrial inspection and production line monitoring. These market types cater to different needs and industries within the Shortwave Infrared Equipment market.</p></p>
