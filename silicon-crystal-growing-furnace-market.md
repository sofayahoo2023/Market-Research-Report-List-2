<p><strong>The Silicon Crystal Growing Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski(CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone(FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Crystal Growing Furnace market consists of two main types of furnaces: Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnaces and Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnaces. CZ Method Furnaces use a crucible to melt silicon and a rotating seed crystal to pull a single crystal ingot. This method is widely used in the industry due to its efficiency and low cost. FZ Method Furnaces, on the other hand, use a high-energy heat source to melt and solidify silicon without the need for a crucible. This method is more expensive but produces higher-quality crystals for specialized applications.</p></p>
