<p><p>Sleep apnea therapeutic devices market includes various types of devices used to treat sleep apnea. The most common type is continuous positive airway pressure (CPAP) devices, which deliver a constant flow of air pressure to keep the airways open during sleep. These devices are effective for most sleep apnea patients. Other types of devices include auto-titrating positive airway pressure (APAP) devices, which automatically adjust the pressure based on individual needs, and bilevel positive airway pressure (BiPAP) devices, which deliver varying pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation. These devices offer alternatives for patients who are unable to tolerate CPAP therapy.</p></p>
