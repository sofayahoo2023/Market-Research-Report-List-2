<p><p>Sodium Carboxymethylcellulose (CMC) is a water-soluble polymer derived from cellulose. It is produced through the chemical modification of natural cellulose using sodium monochloroacetic acid. CMC is widely used in various industries such as food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, personal care, oil and gas, and textiles due to its versatile properties. It acts as a thickening agent, stabilizer, emulsifier, and binder in a wide range of applications.</p><p>The global Sodium Carboxymethylcellulose (CMC) market has been witnessing steady growth in recent years. This can be attributed to the increasing demand for convenience foods, especially in developing regions. CMC finds extensive usage in the food and beverage industry as a thickener, binder, and stabilizer in products like ice creams, dairy products, sauces, dressings, and bakery goods. The demand for CMC is also driven by its widespread application in the pharmaceutical industry, where it is used as a tablet disintegrant, suspending agent, and binder.</p><p>In terms of market analysis and trends, the Sodium Carboxymethylcellulose (CMC) market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
