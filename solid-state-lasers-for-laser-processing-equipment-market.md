<p><p>Solid-state lasers for laser processing equipment are used in various industrial applications such as cutting, welding, marking, and engraving. There are two main types of solid-state lasers: picosecond and nanosecond lasers. Picosecond lasers emit ultra-short pulses (picoseconds) of high energy, allowing for precise and clean processing on materials like metals and semiconductors. On the other hand, nanosecond lasers emit longer pulses (nanoseconds) and are suitable for applications like drilling and surface treatment, offering good processing speed and efficiency. Both types cater to the specific needs of laser processing equipment in different industries.</p></p>
