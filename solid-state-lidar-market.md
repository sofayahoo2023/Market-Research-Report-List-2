<p><p>Solid-state LiDAR is a technology used for depth sensing, mapping, and object detection. MEMS Based Scanning employs tiny mirrors to scan the laser beam and gather data. Phase Array utilizes a network of tiny antennas to direct and receive laser signals, enabling simultaneous scanning without moving parts. Non-Scanning Flash projects laser beams in multiple directions simultaneously, capturing the entire environment in one flash. These types offer advantages like compact size, high reliability, and rapid response, contributing to the growing applications of solid-state LiDAR in various industries.</p></p>
