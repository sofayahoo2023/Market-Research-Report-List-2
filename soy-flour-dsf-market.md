<p><p>Soy Flour, also known as DSF (Defatted Soy Flour), can be classified into two main market types: Full-fat Soy Flour and Defatted Soy Flour. Full-fat Soy Flour is made from whole soybeans and contains all the natural oils found in the soybean. It is a rich source of protein and nutrients. On the other hand, Defatted Soy Flour is produced by removing the soybean oil, resulting in a lower fat content. Both market types have their distinct advantages and uses in various industries such as food processing and baking.</p></p>
