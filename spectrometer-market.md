<p><p>The spectrometer market consists of two main types: atomic spectrometers and molecular spectrometers. Atomic spectrometers are used to analyze the elemental composition of a sample by studying the absorption or emission of light by atoms. They are widely used in various industries for quality control and research purposes. On the other hand, molecular spectrometers are employed to study the molecular composition of a sample by measuring the interaction of light with molecules. They find applications in fields like pharmaceuticals, environmental analysis, and forensic science. Both types of spectrometers play crucial roles in scientific research and industrial processes.</p></p>
