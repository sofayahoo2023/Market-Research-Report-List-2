<p><p>Spherical Microsilica is a type of microsilica that is produced through Sol-Gel Technology and Ion Flame Technology. Sol-Gel Technology involves a chemical process where liquid precursors are transformed into solid materials, resulting in a highly pure and uniform microsilica product. On the other hand, Ion Flame Technology uses the thermal decomposition of silicon tetrachloride gas to generate spherical microsilica particles. Both methods offer distinct advantages in terms of particle size, purity, and uniformity, making them sought-after technologies in the Spherical Microsilica market.</p></p>
