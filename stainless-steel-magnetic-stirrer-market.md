<p><p>The stainless steel magnetic stirrer market includes two main types: thermostatic magnetic stirrer and heated magnetic stirrer. </p><p>Thermostatic magnetic stirrers are designed to maintain a constant temperature during the stirring process. They are commonly used in laboratories for experiments that require precise temperature control.</p><p>Heated magnetic stirrers, on the other hand, not only provide stirring action but also heat the solution being stirred. They are ideal for applications where heating and mixing are required simultaneously, such as in chemical reactions or sample preparations.</p></p>
