<p><p>Stair stepper machines come in two main types: revolving stairs and steppers. Revolving stair machines simulate the motion of climbing actual stairs by using a rotating staircase. This type provides a more realistic workout experience and targets different muscles. Stepper machines, on the other hand, offer a stepping motion similar to climbing stairs but without the revolving staircase feature. These machines are generally smaller, more compact, and often less expensive compared to revolving stair machines. Both types provide effective cardiovascular workouts and allow individuals to improve their leg strength and endurance.</p></p>
