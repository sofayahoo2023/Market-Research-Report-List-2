<p><strong>The Strain Gauge Converters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Current Output</li><li>Voltage Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Strain gauge converters are devices used to measure and convert strains or deformations in various applications. There are two main types of strain gauge converters: current output and voltage output. </p><p>Current output strain gauge converters provide an output signal in the form of a current proportional to the applied strain. This type of converter requires a power supply to function and is suitable for applications requiring long-distance signal transmission.</p><p>On the other hand, voltage output strain gauge converters provide an output signal in the form of a voltage proportional to the applied strain. This type of converter is self-powered and suitable for applications where the distance between the converter and the measurement system is relatively short.</p></p>
