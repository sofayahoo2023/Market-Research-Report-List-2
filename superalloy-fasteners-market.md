<p><p>Superalloy fasteners refer to high-performance fasteners that are made from specific superalloys, known for their remarkable heat resistance and strength. The market for superalloy fasteners is categorized into three main types: nickel-based, iron-based, and cobalt-based superalloys. Nickel-based superalloys are widely used due to their excellent corrosion resistance and thermal stability. Iron-based superalloys are known for their cost-effectiveness and good heat resistance. Cobalt-based superalloys offer exceptional wear resistance and high-temperature strength.
