<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Tablet coating equipment market types include standard coating pans, perforated coating pans, and fluidized bed or air suspension coaters. Standard coating pans are the most commonly used equipment, consisting of a rotating pan where tablets are sprayed with a coating solution. Perforated coating pans have holes to enhance drying and create a more uniform coating. Fluidized bed or air suspension coaters use a stream of air to suspend tablets, allowing for even coating and faster drying. Each type of equipment offers different benefits in terms of coating efficiency and quality.</p></p>
