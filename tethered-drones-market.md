<p><p>The tethered drones market encompasses three types: ground fixed type, vehicle mounted mobile type, and shipborne mobile type. The ground fixed type refers to drones that are operated from a fixed location on the ground, while the vehicle mounted mobile type involves drones that can be transported and operated from a vehicle. The shipborne mobile type, on the other hand, refers to drones that are deployed and operated from a ship.
