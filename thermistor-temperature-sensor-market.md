Contact thermistor temperature sensors are designed to come into direct contact with the object being measured, providing accurate and reliable readings. On the other hand, non-contact thermistor temperature sensors can measure temperature from a distance without physical contact, utilizing infrared technology. Both types of sensors offer unique advantages and can be found in a range of applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
