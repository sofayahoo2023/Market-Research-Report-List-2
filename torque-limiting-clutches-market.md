<p><p>Torque limiting clutches market includes various types: friction type, ball type, and others. Friction type torque limiting clutches utilize the friction generated between the driving and driven elements to limit torque transmission. Ball type torque limiting clutches have small ball bearings or spheres placed between the driving and driven elements, allowing the clutch to slip at a predetermined torque level. Other types of torque limiting clutches may involve the use of springs or magnetic forces to limit torque transmission. These different types offer versatility and cater to various industrial applications.</p></p>
