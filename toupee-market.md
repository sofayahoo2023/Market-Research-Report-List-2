Factors such as increasing prevalence of hair loss, rising disposable income, changing fashion trends, and growing awareness about grooming and personal appearance have contributed to the market's expansion. The men's segment dominates the market, but the women's segment is expected to grow rapidly due to increasing awareness and acceptance of women's hair loss issues.</p><p>Overall, the toupee market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.2% during the forecast period.
