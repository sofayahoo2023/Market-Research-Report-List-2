<p><strong>The Traction Alternators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Brushless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traction alternators are devices used in electric vehicles to generate electrical power for propulsion. In the market, two types of traction alternators can be found: static and brushless. Static traction alternators are designed with brushes that conduct the current between the rotating and stationary parts. On the other hand, brushless traction alternators eliminate the need for brushes by using a magnetic field to generate electricity. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages, and their market demand may vary depending on factors such as cost, efficiency, and durability.</p></p>
