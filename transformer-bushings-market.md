They are designed to withstand high voltage levels, mechanical stress, and environmental conditions. Transformer bushings help in transferring current from the conductor to the ground, providing stability and reliability to the power transmission system.</p><p>The global transformer bushings market is expected to witness steady growth during the forecast period. The rising need for electricity in developing countries, increasing efforts to upgrade existing power infrastructure, and the growing emphasis on renewable energy sources are driving the market growth.
