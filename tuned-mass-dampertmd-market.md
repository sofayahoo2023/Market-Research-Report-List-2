<p><p>The Tuned Mass Damper (TMD) market can be categorized into two types: Vertical TMD and Horizontal TMD. The Vertical TMD is a passive device designed to reduce the vibrations in tall structures caused by wind, earthquakes, or human activities. It consists of a mass that moves vertically to counteract the building's oscillations. On the other hand, the Horizontal TMD is used to mitigate the horizontal vibrations produced by bridges, skyscrapers, or other structures. Its mass moves horizontally to dampen the oscillations and improve the overall stability of the structure.</p></p>
