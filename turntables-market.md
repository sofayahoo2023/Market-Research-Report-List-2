<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Turntables are market types that refer to different mechanisms used for spinning the vinyl record. Direct-drive turntables have the motor directly connected to the center spindle, ensuring consistent speed and better control for DJs. Belt-drive turntables use a belt to connect the motor to the platter, reducing motor noise and vibration for audiophile listening. Idler-wheel turntables use a wheel to transfer the motor's power to the platter, offering durability and stability but may produce more noise. Each market type caters to different user preferences and needs.</p></p>
