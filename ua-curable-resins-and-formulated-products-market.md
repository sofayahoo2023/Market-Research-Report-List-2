<p><p>UA (Urethane Acrylate) Curable Resins and Formulated Products Market consists of various market types. Oligomers are prepolymers with low molecular weight that undergo further polymerization during the curing process. Monomers are small molecules that react with oligomers to form a polymer network. Photoinitiators are chemicals that facilitate polymerization under UV or visible light. Additives are substances added to improve the properties of the resin. Other market types refer to any additional components or products, such as fillers or coatings, used in UA curable resins and formulated products.</p></p>
