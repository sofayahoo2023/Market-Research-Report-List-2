<p><strong>The Ultra-portable Projectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DLP Projectors</li><li>LCOS Projectors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultra-portable projectors market is classified into three types: DLP Projectors, LCOS Projectors, and Others. DLP Projectors use a technology called Digital Light Processing to produce images by reflecting light off tiny mirrors. LCOS Projectors rely on Liquid Crystal on Silicon technology to display images by reflecting light through liquid crystals on a reflective surface. The "Others" category encompasses projectors that employ different technologies such as LCoQ, LED, and LCD. These types of projectors offer compact size and lightweight design, making them highly portable for various applications.</p></p>
