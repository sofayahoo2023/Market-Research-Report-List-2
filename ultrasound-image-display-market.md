<p><p>The ultrasound image display market is categorized into two types: monochrome display and color display. Monochrome displays provide images in shades of gray, allowing for a clear depiction of anatomical structures. They are typically used in basic ultrasound examinations. On the other hand, color displays offer a wider range of colors and are used for more advanced imaging, such as Doppler ultrasound, as they allow for the visualization of blood flow and other dynamic information. Both types of displays play a crucial role in medical imaging, providing essential information to healthcare professionals.</p></p>
