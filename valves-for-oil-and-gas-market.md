<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Gate valves are used to control the flow of oil and gas by raising or lowering a gate that blocks or allows the passage of fluids. Control valves manage the flow rate, pressure, and temperature, ensuring smooth operation of the system. Ball valves have a rotating ball with a hole that controls the fluid flow. Butterfly valves consist of a disc that pivots to regulate the flow. Plug valves feature a cylindrical or conical plug to control the flow by rotating it within the valve. These valves are crucial in the oil and gas industry to maintain efficient operations.</p></p>
