<p><p>Water hardness test kits are tools used to determine the mineral content in water, indicating the water's hardness level. There are two main types of water hardness test kits available in the market. The first type is the test tube kit, which includes a series of liquid reagents. These reagents are added to a water sample in a test tube, and a color change indicates the level of hardness. The second type is the test strip kit, consisting of strips coated with chemicals. The strips are dipped into the water, and the colors that develop correspond to the hardness level.</p></p>
