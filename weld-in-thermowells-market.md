<p><p>The weld-in thermowells market includes three main types: weld-in socketed type straight thermowells, weld-in socketed type tapered thermowells, and weld-in socketed type stepped thermowells. Weld-in socketed type straight thermowells have a uniform diameter throughout their length, allowing for easy installation. Weld-in socketed type tapered thermowells have a gradually decreasing diameter, providing better protection against corrosion and vibration. Weld-in socketed type stepped thermowells have multiple sections with different diameters, providing enhanced resistance to thermal shock and process fluid characteristics. These thermowells serve as protective enclosures for temperature sensors in industrial processes.</p></p>
