<p><p>Wound irrigation devices are used to clean wounds during medical procedures. Bulb syringes are hand-held devices that create suction to irrigate wounds. Piston syringes use a piston to create pressure and flush wounds. Pressure canisters deliver a continuous stream of irrigation fluid through a nozzle. Whirlpool agitators create a swirling motion in a solution to cleanse wounds. Whirlpool hose sprayers use pressurized water to cleanse wounds. Irrigation fluid market refers to the industry that supplies the various types of fluids used in wound irrigation devices.</p></p>
