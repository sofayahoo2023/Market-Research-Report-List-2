<p><p>X-ray systems for industrial inspection are used to examine objects or materials for flaws, defects, or inconsistencies. Two main types of X-ray systems used in this market are Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). DR type systems use digital detectors to capture X-ray images, offering faster processing times and better image quality. CT type systems, on the other hand, create 3D images by taking multiple X-ray images around an object from different angles, allowing for a detailed analysis of internal structures and defects.</p></p>
